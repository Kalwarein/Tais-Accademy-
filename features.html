App Features

Discover all the powerful features that make Sheikh Tais Academy app your perfect educational companion

Daily Timetable Access

View your complete class schedule, including subjects, timings, and classroom locations, all in one place. Never miss a class again with our intuitive timetable interface.

  • Real-time schedule updates
  • Classroom location mapping
  • Teacher contact information
  • Offline access available

Announcements & Updates

Stay informed with real-time school announcements, important notices, and event updates. Get push notifications for urgent messages.

  • Push notifications
  • Category-wise filtering
  • Archive of past announcements
  • Priority message highlighting

Results and Grades

Access your exam results, grades, and academic performance reports instantly. Track your progress with detailed analytics and insights.

  • Instant result notifications
  • Grade trend analysis
  • Subject-wise performance
  • Downloadable report cards

Assignments Overview

Keep track of all your assignments, submission deadlines, and project requirements. Never miss a deadline with our smart reminder system.

  • Deadline reminders
  • Assignment submission portal
  • Progress tracking
  • Teacher feedback system

School Calendar

Never miss important dates with our comprehensive school calendar featuring events, holidays, exam dates, and special activities.

  • Event notifications
  • Holiday schedules
  • Exam timetables
  • Personal calendar sync

Important Documents

Download and access important school documents, forms, and PDFs anytime, anywhere. All documents are organized and easily searchable.

  • Document categorization
  • Search functionality
  • Offline document access
  • Version control
