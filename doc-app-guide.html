Getting Started

How to use the Sheikh Tais Academy

1

Download the App

Download the Sheikh Tais Academy app from our official website or through the provided APK file.

Make sure to download from official sources only

2

Install & Launch

Install the app on your Android device and launch it. Make sure you have enabled installation from unknown sources if needed

Enable "Unknown Sources" in your device settings if prompted

3

Create Account

Signup Using your email and a password including your name + Account type it includes student,staff,parent choose select your Account type fill all fields and signup.

4

Verify Profile

Complete your profile verification by clicking the button sent to your email to access all features. and now signin with the credentials you used to create your Account

note: once you clicked the button in the email sent to you exit the email app and return to the tais app and signin

Troubleshooting

Common Issues

App won't load or crashes frequently

Try clearing the app cache, restarting your device, or reinstalling the app. Ensure you have sufficient storage space and a stable internet connection.

Can't log in with my credentials

Double-check your student ID and password. If you've forgotten your password, use the "Forgot Password" option or contact the school office for assistance.

Notifications not working

Check your device's notification settings and ensure the app has permission to send notifications. Also verify your notification preferences within the app settings.
