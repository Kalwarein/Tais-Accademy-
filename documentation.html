Mobile App Guide

Complete guide on how to download, install, and use the Sheikh Tais Academy mobile application.

Getting Started

  • Download the app from our downloads page
  • Install the APK file on your Android device
  • Create your account using school-provided credentials
  • Complete your profile setup

Key Features

  • Dashboard: Overview of your academic information
  • Timetable: View your class schedule and timing
  • Grades: Check your exam results and grades
  • Announcements: Stay updated with school news
  • Calendar: Important dates and events
  • Documents: Access important school documents

Troubleshooting

  • If app won't install: Enable "Unknown Sources" in device settings
  • Login issues: Contact school administration for credential verification
  • App crashes: Clear app cache or reinstall the application
  • No internet: Some features require active internet connection

School Rules & Regulations

Important school policies, rules, and regulations that every student and parent should know.

General Conduct

  • Students must arrive at school by 8:00 AM
  • Proper school uniform is mandatory
  • Mobile phones should be on silent during classes
  • Respect teachers, staff, and fellow students

Academic Policies

  • Minimum 75% attendance required
  • Assignment submission deadlines must be met
  • Cheating or plagiarism is strictly prohibited
  • Make-up exams only for valid medical reasons

Disciplinary Actions

  • First warning: Verbal counseling
  • Second offense: Written warning to parents
  • Repeated violations: Suspension or expulsion

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions and answers about Sheikh Tais Academy services and policies.

How do I get login credentials for the mobile app?

Login credentials are provided by the school administration during admission. Contact the office if you haven't received your credentials.

What should I do if I forget my password?

Use the "Forgot Password" option in the app or contact the school office for password reset assistance.

How can parents access their child's information?

Parents receive separate login credentials to access their child's academic information, attendance, and school communications.

Is the app available for iOS devices?

Currently, the app is available only for Android devices. iOS version is in development and will be released soon.
